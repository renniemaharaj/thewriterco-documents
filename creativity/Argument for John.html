Is life the fruit of love?

God is love; the Love.

Now "In the beginning was the Word,

and the Word was with God, and the Word was God."

Lord Jesus, that Word and Son of God:

claimed to be the Way,

The Truth, and the Life.

The fruit of God is the Life, the Son of God.

So for as long as God was, Life was, which was always.

Therefore, the begotten fruit of God, the Life, was and is God.
